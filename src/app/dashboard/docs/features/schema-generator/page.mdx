# Schema Generator

<Subtitle>Generate structured data markup optimized for AI Overview visibility.</Subtitle>

## Overview

The Schema Generator creates JSON-LD structured data designed to help search engines and AI models understand your content more effectively. Well-implemented structured data makes it easier for AI systems to extract facts, relationships, and key information from your pages — increasing the likelihood of being cited in AI Overviews.

## Supported Schema Types

Citatra supports generating structured data for the following schema types:

- **FAQPage** — for pages with question-and-answer content
- **HowTo** — for tutorials, guides, and step-by-step instructions
- **Article** — for blog posts, news articles, and editorial content
- **Product** — for product pages with pricing, availability, and reviews
- **LocalBusiness** — for location-based businesses with address and contact details
- **Organization** — for company-level information including logo, social profiles, and contact points

## How to Use

1. **Select a page** from your tracked URLs or enter a new URL
2. **Choose a schema type** that matches the page's content
3. **Review the pre-filled fields** — Citatra analyzes the page and populates fields automatically where possible
4. **Customize the output** — edit any fields to ensure accuracy and completeness
5. **Copy the JSON-LD** — paste the generated markup into your page's `<head>` section or deploy via Google Tag Manager

## Best Practices

- Use **FAQPage** schema for pages that answer common questions — these align naturally with AI Overview queries
- Use **HowTo** schema for tutorials and process-oriented content
- Keep structured data **consistent with visible page content** — mismatches can result in penalties
- **Validate your markup** with the [Google Rich Results Test](https://search.google.com/test/rich-results) after implementation
- Update structured data whenever page content changes significantly

## AI Overview Optimization

Structured data helps AI models parse your content with greater precision. When an AI system encounters well-structured JSON-LD, it can more confidently extract specific facts, attribute information to your domain, and determine whether your content answers a given query. This doesn't replace quality content, but it removes friction from the extraction process.

<Info>Schema markup doesn't guarantee AI Overview citations, but it helps AI models parse your content more accurately.</Info>
